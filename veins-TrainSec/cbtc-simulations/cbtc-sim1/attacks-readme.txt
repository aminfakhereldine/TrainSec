Attack types
------------

1)"RANDOM_TSM_DELAY": a train adds a random delay offset to the TSM sending time

2)"RANDOM_WOM_DELAY" a WSU adds a random delay offset to the WOM sending time

3)"RANDOM_POSITION_OFFSET": a train adds to its real posx a random offset from a predefined range
	
4)"RANDOM_SPEED_OFFSET": a train adds to its real speed a random offset from a predefined range
	
5)"RANDOM_LMA_OFFSET": a WSU adds to the real LMA a random offset from a predefined range

6)"CHANGE_SPEED": a train changes its speed during the cruising phase
